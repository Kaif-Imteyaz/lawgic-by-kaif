Project Name: Lawgic
Version: 0.1.0
Private: true

Description:
Lawgic is an AI-powered legal assistant that leverages the latest advancements in AI and machine learning to help legal professionals and individuals analyze legal documents. The platform provides services such as summarizing legal documents, predicting potential judgments, and identifying relevant statutes, thereby streamlining the legal process.
